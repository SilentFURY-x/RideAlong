**RideAlong** is a smart carpooling platform designed for university students to coordinate rides to airports and railway stations. It eliminates the chaos of WhatsApp groups, helping students save money and reduce their carbon footprint.

---

## üßê The Problem
Every holiday season, students face the same struggle:
* üì± **Spam:** College WhatsApp groups are flooded with hundreds of "Anyone going to the airport?" messages.
* üìâ **Inefficiency:** Requests get buried in chat history, leading to missed opportunities.
* üí∏ **Cost:** Many end up traveling alone and paying 4x the price because they couldn't find a partner in time.

## üí° The Solution
**RideAlong** replaces the noise with a clean, centralized dashboard.
* **Structured Listings:** No more scrolling. Filter rides by Date, Time, and Destination.
* **Privacy First:** Connect with verified student emails. No phone numbers shared until a request is accepted.
* **Real-Time Coordination:** A "Request & Accept" system ensures seats are booked fairly.

## üöÄ Features

### 1. üè† Host a Ride
Going to the airport? Create a ride card in seconds:
* Set Source & Destination
* Choose Date & Time
* Define Available Seats

### 2. üîç Explore & Filter
Find a ride that matches your schedule:
* **Smart Search:** Filter by "Airport", "Station", or specific city names.
* **Date Picker:** See only what's relevant to your travel plans.

### 3. ü§ù Request System
* **Joiners:** Send a "Request to Join" with one click.
* **Hosts:** Review incoming requests on a dedicated dashboard. Accept or Reject based on seat availability.
* **Status Updates:** Real-time feedback (Pending ‚Üí Accepted/Rejected).

### 4. üåç Environmental Impact
By filling empty seats, RideAlong significantly reduces the number of cars on the road.
> *Filling one 4-seater cab takes 3 extra cars off the road. That‚Äôs a **75% reduction in carbon emissions** for just one trip.*
